Tribute to Rani Chennamma of Kittur

The Warrior Queen

The Fearless Leader

Rani Chennamma was the queen of the princely state of Kittur in Karnataka. She was born in 1778 and became the ruler of Kittur after her husband's death. Known for her bravery and leadership, she fought against the British East India Company's annexation of her kingdom.

In 1824, when the British attempted to take control of Kittur, Rani Chennamma led her army in a fierce battle against them. Despite facing a much larger and better-equipped enemy force, she displayed remarkable courage and strategic acumen. Though ultimately captured and imprisoned, her resistance inspired others to continue the fight for independence.
